WORD GUESS

Multiplayer Word Game

How to Play

  • Create a session or join one with a session code.
  • Choose a word length — 4, 5, 6, 7, or 8 letters.
  • Each round, all players submit their guess simultaneously.
  • Green — correct letter, correct position.
  • Yellow — correct letter, wrong position.
  • Gray — letter not in the word.
  • The player who guesses the word in the fewest rounds wins!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need an account?
    No. Just enter your name and join or create a session instantly.
  • How many players can join?
    Up to 16 players per session.
  • What word lengths are available?
    4, 5, 6, 7, or 8 letters — chosen by the session host.
  • Is the game free?
    Yes, Word Guess is completely free to play.
